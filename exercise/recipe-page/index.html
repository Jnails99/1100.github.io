MAKING THE CAKE

The best part about loaf cakes is how easy they are to make! It comes together so quickly and can even be whipped up all in one bowl. Let’s break it apart into two sections, the dry ingredients and the wet ingredients.

Here is what you need for the dry ingredients:

  • all-purpose flour
  • baking powder
  • baking soda
  • salt
  • lemon instant pudding

THE GLAZE

Learning how to make a glaze is a great recipe to have under your belt if you love to bake a lot! It can be drizzled on cakes, cookies, donuts, and so much more. The best part is it usually only needs two ingredients at its core and then you can add in other optional ingredients to bring in different flavors. Here is what you need:

  • confectioners sugar
  • lemon juice
  • whole milk
  • vanilla extract
